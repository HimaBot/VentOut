Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Here’s exactly what we do and do not collect.

What We Collect:
  • Anonymized technical logs (for security)
What We Don’t Collect:
  • No names
  • No recordings
  • No saved chats or audio
Cookies:

Used only for smooth website operation—never for advertising.

Encryption:

All audio is encrypted using secure WebRTC technology.

Third Parties:

Used sparingly and securely (e.g., Stripe for optional donations).
